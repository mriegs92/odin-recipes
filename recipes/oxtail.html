Matt's Famous Oxtail Stew

Oxtail:
The most succulent, tender, and delicious cut that isn't probably isn't in your refrigerator

Ingredients:

  • 2lbs oxtails
  • ketchup
  • mustard
  • cilantro
    • organic
  • cayenne
  • soy sauce
  • chili powder
  • scotch bonnet pepper

Prep:

  1. Get your mise en place
  2. chop the fresh ingredients(ie. peppers and cilantro)
  3. in a large plastic bag mix all the ingredients together (add each to taste) and let them marinate in the fridge overnight
  4. When ready to prepare remove the oxtails from the bag and place in a pressure cooker on the "brown" setting (DO NOT DISCARD THE MARINADE), cook 10 mins or until fat becomes golden brown and a little crispy.
  5. Add the marinade to the pressure cooker anf let simmer 5 mins
  6. Then add water so that theres sbout a half inch of water covers the oxtails
  7. Pressure cook for 1 hour so that the meat is fall-off-the- bone tender
  8. Enjoy!
