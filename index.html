Brazilian Lasagna Recipe

This is my favorite dish that my wife makes. Before I met her italian style lasagna was one of my favorite dishes. In fact, I didn't know there was anything out there but italian lasagna. However, after being introduced to the brazilian version, with its inclusion of ham, a white sauce and brazilian cheeses my preferences changed forever. Matt's Oxtail Stew

Oxtails have long been my favorite food. It coulde be Jamaican, Trinidadian, Brazilian or Mexican style and it will have my love. Given my vast experience trying out different cultures takes on Oxtail Stew I figured I'd make one the takes the best attributes from each.

Nothing else compares to the davory golden brown goodness of Oxtails Stew. Beware! The mix of savory, spicy, and slightly sweet may throw you into a feeding frenzy!
